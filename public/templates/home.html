What is captivio?

Captivio is a social media marketing company specialising in bringing brands and customers closer together, allowing everyone to be part of the event.

We gather social media as its created during live events and creatively curate and edit the data to enhance customer experiences and bring them closer to the action.

Captivio is unique in that it focuses from your event outwards as opposed to trying to capture generic detail around the event.

Captivio doesn't just let you be at the event ... it lets you be part of it!
